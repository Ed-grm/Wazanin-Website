About Wazanin

We are dedicated to facilitating employment opportunities between Ethiopia and Saudi Arabia, ensuring a seamless process for both job seekers and employers.

Your Trusted Employment Partner

Founded in 2015, Wazanin has established itself as a leading recruitment agency specializing in connecting skilled Ethiopian professionals with reputable Saudi employers. With a deep understanding of both markets, we bridge cultural and logistical gaps to create successful employment partnerships.

Our team of experts handles every aspect of the recruitment process, from candidate sourcing and vetting to visa processing and post-placement support. We pride ourselves on our ethical approach and commitment to fair employment practices.
